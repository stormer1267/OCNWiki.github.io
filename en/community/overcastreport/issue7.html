Defenders: Age of Ares

Things are heating up for Defenders: Age of Ares! All competing teams now have access to their practice servers. BrettOgden recently posted about the 6 team groups for the qualifying rounds, which will be happening next weekend. However, if you're on a team, you should keep an eye out later today for a very important post!

Staff UHC

Yesterday, a large group of staff members waged war in a bout of teams of 3 UHC. Laughter was had, blood was shed, and only one team reigned victors. But who was it? For now, that is confidential information, but at least one member of every team (except one) recorded their POV. You can expect a release schedule to be posted in the upcoming week, where you can watch along with the rest of the community to see how your staff fared. In the meantime, place your bets!

Development News

This week, you found quite a few bugs to keep the devs busy, but they still managed to get out a few new features. These bugs included: being able to view and remove items from observers' inventories, being able to shoot one's self with arrows (which shows up in your kill feed), and void or lava damage not counting when team spleefed. All of these have been taken care of, and now we are free to move onto the new features for the week!

  • First up is thanks to our translation team pushing forward with the localization of the server. The "Howto" book has been the newest item to receive the translation treatment, but it can't be pushed to the servers quite yet.
  • There have been some improvements to the way observer hotbar tools are given.
  • The /updatemap command is a brand new tool for map developers and mapmakers in residence. It allows them to make edits to the map on their server and push these edits directly to our Dropbox. This allows edits to be made much quicker, easier, and more efficiently.

New Maps

Three new maps were added this week:

  • Xion, a CTW map by rockymine and Avaleer.
  • Gleon, a Blitz map by Qixit.
  • Artext, a CTF mixed with KotH map by LArrowHead and DoomRobotBoy.

Map Contest

Our second map making contest is coming to a close, but due to some unforeseen circumstances, it's taking longer than expected to finish up testing and judging. There are only 3 maps left to fix, but public testing of the maps will begin this week. Keep an eye on the forums to make sure you can be involved with the testing.

Staff News

For the first time since we started the /Report, there have been no staffing changes at all this week! However, we have been very busy behind the scenes going through the final selection process for a small batch of new Junior Moderators. Watch this space next week for (possible) news...

New Bot for OCN Slack Users

Nimbus is a simple open-source chat bot for the popular Slack team chat tool, made by our own bcbwilla and Plastix. Its main function is to display the title and content of forum threads when people link them in chat, and it was built primarily for use in the staff Slack team, but we thought it might be of interest to other Slack fans in the community too.
