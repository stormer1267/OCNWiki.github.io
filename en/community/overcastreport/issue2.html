Arcade Update

ElectroidFilms says it's time to talk Arcade, and boy was he right! In his Status Update, he talks about the goal of the arcade, the requirements for Arcade maps, and even some current maps in development. Now that Cubist has finished the Free for All module, the fun can begin. Our Map Developers will be working hard on making maps for the Arcade, but as always, we turn to the community to help us innovate and progress. We can't wait to see what you will come up with, and how much fun we will have in the process.

Weekly and Monthly Stats Are Back!

Stat-fans rejoice... time-windowed statistics are back! Earlier in the week, Apple pushed out an update that re-enabled this feature which was turned off a while ago. You can now search and view statistics using three time filters - 24 hours, past week and all time. Have fun exploring the data.

Development News

The servers have been upgraded to Minecraft 1.8.7, which includes some minor features and bug fixes from Mojang. A Bukkit bug that broke icarus cannons was also fixed; however, the upgrade introduced a new TNT bug that is causing spread cannons to occasionally self-destruct, and developers are looking into it. Please let us know on our issue tracker if you're aware of any other TNT-related bugs - and if possible, provide clear explanations (video would be ideal).

Also, YukonAppleGeek is now back to working full time on development, so expect to see more fun stuff happening in this area this summer.

Staff News

This week BrettOgden extended his growing list of forum badges by becoming our newest Map Developer!

Also, we said goodbye to one of our longest-serving moderators — Munchkin99hdpvp (better known as MoNoO) has decided to step down after almost 3 years on the staff. We're sad to see him go, and wish him well for the future.

New Mini Rotations

Thanks to a final push by Tywnis, the long-awaited Mini rotation update went live yesterday! So enjoy the new variety of maps, and check out Strangey's announcement post if you want to read more about the changes.

In other news, there are a few forum topics by map developers looking for specific feedback on rotations and maps at the moment:

  • Anthonny is looking for feedback on Valley of the Dead.
  • Strangey would like your opinions on a bunch of proposals to improve Conquer and Deathmatch maps.
  • mitchiii_ is asking for community feedback and suggestions on Trinus.

Map News

One new map hit the repo this week — The Last Sunset II is a CTF map by DoomRobotBoy and ogel.

As well as that, there have been lots of updates to existing maps. In particular, PepsiDog's much loved mini DTM Antiquis got a big update which fixes trading and also adds new crafting recipes so you can upgrade your tools without heading back to spawn.

June Tournament Map Making Event

We are pleased to announce a Tournament Map Making Event! This event will be a test of who can make the best Capture the Flag, or King of the Flag map, especially for tournament play.

There will be two weeks for maps to be made, and then the testing and judging will begin. The lead builder of the top 3 maps will earn a "Map Residency" to further develop and polish their map before being accepted into the Tournament 10 map pool by the Event Coordinators. This is the first time the community has had the opportunity to earn a spot in a tournament's map pool, so we can't wait to see the level of competition that will come through.

Rules, dates, and more specifics can be found in the announcement thread. Best of luck to any and all map makers who decide to join!

Interested in Helping to Referee Tournaments?

A reminder that the event coordinators are still accepting referee applications until June 16th (next Tuesday). Any applications sent in after this time may not be considered, so be sure to get yours in as soon as you can.

The Last Life Postponed to Next Weekend

SheriffSoco announced yesterday that the unofficial Last Life blitz tournament has been postponed by a week due to unforseen issues. It will now take place next weekend, but we're sure it will be worth the wait.
