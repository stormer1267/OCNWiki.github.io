Development News

Several new exciting modules have been made live by Cubist. The shield module gives players a recharging shield of absorption hearts and works like a kit. This means the shield can be upgraded or given like any other kit. Shield regeneration is based on a timer that resets every time damage is taken. The amount of time to regenerate can be set by the map maker, as can the amount of hearts.

The terrain module brings natural world generation to PGM, previously impossible due to plugin limitations. The module allows for natural terrain to be generated by disabling the empty chunk generator and using a randomized seed for each match. Generation can be modified by adding a custom level.dat allowing for variable sea levels, huge mountains, and more.

Meanwhile, the structure module can place pre-built structures in the world based on various triggers, such as a team capturing a point. Dynamic structures are cloned into the playing area from another location in the map. Cubist put up a quick demonstration video to help mapmakers get a feel for what this module can do.

These new modules are all still under development, but will be documented on docs.oc.tc soon.
