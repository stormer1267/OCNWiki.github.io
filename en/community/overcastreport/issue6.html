Map Contest Update

The map making contest is starting to wrap up, our MapDevs have been hard at work fixing the most of the submitted maps. We expect public testing to begin very soon and final scores to be posted. Thank you for your patience through the whole process.

Development News

This week, we didn't see a whole lot of significant pushes to the oc.tc/revisions page, but the devs spent a large part of the week refactoring. For those of you who don't know what refactoring is, Cubist explained it well in /Report #3 - "When code gets [messy], smart developers will do something called "refactoring", which essentially means changing how the code works without changing what it does. Because we now understand exactly what the code needs to do, we can write it much better than we could back when we were feeling out new features one at a time. Software projects that are very large and/or old usually require constant refactoring just to keep them alive."

Rotation Changes and Updates

This week we only had one change to the rotations, Ender Blast has been removed from the repo at the request of its authors, Beanbutt and EditorInChief.

Map Updates

Over the past week, we've seen a lot of updates to current maps but no new ones added. This is a side effect of our upcoming event Defenders: Age of Ares's map pool and the map making contest. Both events take up a lot of the MapDev's time.

Staff News

Daiquiriri (DiamondAx) has proven that he is still completely capable of being a full moderator, and has, just yesterday, been promoted!

EditorInChief has been added as a referee, he may be a late addition but we're excited to have him back on our team.

Also, keep your eye on the staff page for even more staff news in the near future.

Prepairing for Age of Ares

The tournament is starting to heat up, expect for teams to be accepted today (July 12th) and the groups / schedule posted tomorrow (July 13th). khazhyk has asked you to check your emails if you sent refs@oc.tc an email recently regarding changes to your registration.
