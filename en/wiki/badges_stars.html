Discontinued Badges

Livestreamer

This was given to people who helped live stream Overcast Network tournaments and events. Live streamers still exist, but they don't get a forum badge.


Referee

This was given to people who helped referee Overcast Network tournaments and events. They now get the Ref badge seen above.


Donor

Before the donation revamp, players who donated at least $10 to the server got this badge. This donator package gave all the same perks as the current shop packages. Every player who had this Donor rank got the 'heart' badge on the website after the donation system revamp.


Preppy Nazi

This badge was given to Celoxia for one day. Not much is known about it other than it gave this user no special perks other than the badge on the website.


Serenity USA

Serenity USA was a creative build server for oc.tc. This badge no longer exist, neither does the server. The admins of the server were given a blue Serenity USA badge on the website.


Inactive Moderator

This was given to moderators who became inactive. They don't get any abilities and they have to ask a senior moderator or admin to become a moderator again. This rank no longer exist. If a staff member becomes inactive, they are usually just removed from the staff team.
